ZeroTier allows you to access your Asustor device from anywhere in the world without the need for port forwarding or cloud services. ZeroTier establishes a direct and encrypted connection to your device even when you aren't on your LAN. Some initial setup is required and is detailed below:

ZeroTier does not yet provide a GUI for Asustor devices. To use the CLI:

1) Using your (admin) account, enable SSH:

 - Services -> Terminal -> Enable SSH (set to port 22)

2) From a computer, open a terminal and SSH into your Asustor device:

 - ssh admin@your_asustor_device_ip

3) Join a network

 - zerotier-cli join your_network_id

4) Go to my.zerotier.com and authorize your Asustor device to join your network.

5) Done!

If you do not yet have a network ID, go to my.zerotier.com, create an account and then create a new network and use its ID in the previous command.
